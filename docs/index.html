Hi there!

I'm Parthiv, a Platform Engineer/Site Reliability Engineer with a decade of experience in the field.   • Writings on Platform Engineering/SRE/DevOps
  • My coding projects in Python/Golang
  • My learning notes and self-paced tutorials as I embark on learning new things
  • ...and whatever else comes to mind

Currently, all of these are clustered under a common "Posts" heading, but as the list grows I plan to organize them into more appropriate sections.
